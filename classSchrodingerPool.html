Detailed Description

Manages a pool of paths with a limited lifespan, computes reduced costs, and filters paths based on their reduced costs.

This class is designed to manage a collection of paths, each associated with an iteration when it was added. Paths have a limited lifespan, defined by max_live_time, after which they are removed from the pool. The class also provides functionality to compute reduced costs for paths and filter paths based on their reduced costs.
